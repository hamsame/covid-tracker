Covid-19 Advice & Stats

Covid-19 is a global disease that first broke out in Wuhan, China. It is highly infectious and is caused by a recently discovered form of coronavirus. Some people infected with Covid-19 are impacted more than others meaning some might end up needing ventilators while others would not. We have been provided with advice that can prevent us from contracting and spreading the virus.

  • The core advice is to:
  • Wear face coverings
  • Stay at home when you can
  • Self isolate if you have symptoms
  • Maintain a distance of 2m from others
  • Avoid physical contact
  • Stay away from large gatherings
  • For more advice head to the WHO website
